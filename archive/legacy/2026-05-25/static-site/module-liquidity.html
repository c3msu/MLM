About This Module

The Liquidity module monitors the availability of money in the financial system. It tracks key metrics including M2 money supply, Federal Reserve balance sheet changes, and overnight reverse repurchase agreements. High liquidity scores indicate abundant capital availability, while low scores may signal tightening conditions.
